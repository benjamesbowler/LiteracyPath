# Guided Story Batch Plan

Date: 2026-05-25

## Round 1 Scope

Round 1 produces 50 controlled Guided Reading books:

- 5 fiction books per level A-E
- 5 nonfiction books per level A-E
- 25 fiction books total
- 25 nonfiction books total

Current active books remain temporary starter content until Round 1 books are written, rendered, validated, and approved.
