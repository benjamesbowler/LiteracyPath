# Curated Public-Domain Guided Reading Books

Generated: 2026-05-25T23:40:21.897Z

## Selection Methodology

- Prioritized public-domain sources with short early-reader structures, illustrations where available, and classroom-safe themes.
- Preferred primers, antique school readers, animal stories, nursery/rhyme texts, and short classic picture books.
- Avoided chapter books, dense Victorian prose, culturally unsafe material, highly religious/preachy material, and scary/violent selections for independent K-2 reading.
- Some collection sources are included only as future excerpt/adaptation candidates and must be reviewed story-by-story before activation.
