# Public-Domain Story Source List

Date: 2026-05-24

This list is for source inspiration only. LiteracyPath should not copy raw public-domain text directly into the app. Each story should be rewritten as an original, leveled, child-friendly guided reading adaptation with fresh app text, fresh image prompts, and matching narration.

Public-domain notes below are focused on original older source material that is generally public domain in the United States. Modern retellings, translations, illustrations, recordings, or branded adaptations may still be copyrighted and must not be copied.
