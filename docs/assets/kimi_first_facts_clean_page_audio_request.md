- Clear American English.
- Neutral soft light female voice.
- Child-friendly and natural for K-2 guided reading.
- No music.
- No sound effects.
- No intro.
- No instruction text.
- Do NOT say "slowly and clearly".
- Do NOT say page numbers.
- Do NOT spell words.
- Do NOT add any extra words.
- Page audio must contain only the exact page text.
- Full-book audio must contain only the exact book text in order, with natural pauses between pages.
