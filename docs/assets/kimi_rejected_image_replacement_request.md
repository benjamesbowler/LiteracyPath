## Non-Negotiable Global Image Rules

- Single centered target object only unless the word itself is a standard single-concept compound object such as fire truck, police car, traffic light, jump rope, video camera, or measuring cup.
- Plain pure white background only (#ffffff).
- No shadow.
- No glow, aura, sparkles, confetti, decorative shapes, magical effects, or fantasy backgrounds.
- No rainbow coloring unless the target word is literally rainbow.
- Use natural/original colors for the real object.
- No faces, eyes, smiles, arms, or legs on objects, fruit, food, vehicles, clothing, tools, letters, or household items.
- Animals and people may have normal natural faces because they naturally have faces.
- No embedded text, labels, captions, speech bubbles, or watermarks.
- No dark, black, patterned, scenic, or cluttered backgrounds.
- The object must be immediately recognizable to a kindergarten child at thumbnail size.
- Use one consistent clean cartoon educational flashcard style across the whole pack.

## Target-Word Rule

Do not create image assets for weak phrase targets that are not real single target objects. `zoo gate` is not acceptable. Compound terms are acceptable only when they name one familiar thing, such as `fire truck`, `police car`, `traffic light`, or `jump rope`.
