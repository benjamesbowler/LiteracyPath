## Replacement Guidance

- Prefer one-word targets for Initial Sounds whenever possible.
- Common single-concept compounds can stay if the image is unambiguous, for example fire truck, police car, traffic light, jump rope, video camera, and measuring cup.
- Avoid location/object phrases such as zoo gate, scene phrases such as quiet room, object-part phrases such as yo-yo string, adjective phrases such as zesty lemon, and ambiguous labels such as yellow jacket.
- Kimi should not improvise new words. Codex must update the word bank first, then Kimi should render the exact target path requested.
