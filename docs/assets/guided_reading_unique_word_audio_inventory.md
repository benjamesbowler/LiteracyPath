## Notes

- Deduping uses lowercase normalized word forms.
- Contractions and possessives are preserved as spoken units and flagged for review.
- Hyphenated compounds are preserved as spoken units when they appear that way in the book text.
- Proper nouns and character names are included because students tap and hear those words in Guided Reading.
- Repeated words should be generated once only, then reused across every book.
